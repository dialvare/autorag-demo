Pizza Bank - Account Policies (2026)

1. Youth Account:
The Youth Account is designed for customers between 18 and 30 years old. 
It has no maintenance or administration fees. 
It allows free national transfers and includes a debit card with no issuance fee.

2. Premium Account:
The Premium Account requires direct depositing a payroll of over 2500 euros per month.
It has a maintenance fee of 15 euros per month if the payroll requirement is not met.
It offers free international transfers and an assigned personal manager.
